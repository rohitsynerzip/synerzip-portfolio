iToi

AN UX PROJECT BY SYNERZIP

Overview

iTOi’s Optical Lens Technology makes it possible for you to look into your client’s eyes, which gives the visual experience necessary to convey your objectives and connect on a personal level.

01 Discover

iTOi booths are amazing and there are so many things you can do with them. The only pain-point Gabriel (VP Product and Operations, iTOi) reported us was “nonlinear purchase process “. However, after a thorough analysis. we found and reported multiple issues and reasons affecting customer’s decision during purchase process.

The brainstorming made it pretty clear that iTOi require a strong, appealing and communicating website. But of course, purchase and payment process should be as simple (and quick) as possible.

02 Define

We defined the approach with guided research to enhance overall user experience by increasing usability and availability of most relevant information about products and subscriptions. From landing page to payment-processing page, flow of information is relevant and information architecture is lean. Usability was further enhanced with added features of saving cart stats; every time a user take action.

03 Design

The design is user-centered, modern, clean, and full responsive in nature. Fine line-icons, pleasing colors, appealing polygon-backgrounds have been used while keeping accessibility in mind.

Desktop Design of iTOi Website
Buy Booth Page, iTOi
Responsive Website

04 Develop

  • Responsive Framework: Twitter Bootstrap
  • Backend-Development: Ruby on Rails
  • Payment Processing: PayPal

05 Deploy

The connection to iTOi site uses a strong protocol version and cipher suite, hosted on secure cloud with full https connection and is supported by all html5 enabled browsers.

06 Drive

We have used power of google analytics while performing prior research and have continued to use the same for new website. We are excited to announce that iTOi website will soon go live. :)

CrowdSolveit

AN UX PROJECT BY SYNERZIP

Overview

CrowdSolveIt is a social networking platform. The idea is to give social entrepreneurs access to the collective intelligence and experience of a vast number of users in order to solve complex problems that affect our world.It is a platform with a purpose to help world by the people, for the people and of the people. The intention is to crowdsolve the issues based on humanitarian grounds with the help of people round the globe.

The objective of the project was to provide a platform where people can come and post their issues faced while solving any problem. In the same way experts can come and provide their inputs and help a poster.

01 Discover

Unlike regular forums this was a tough and complex one. The information was so distributed and yet to be coupled logically so that it is easier for the user to understand and reciprocate. In the initial stage we just received a brief document with client’s idea and expectations which we were expected to be detailed out and derive business goals from it. We remotely conducted some stakeholder’s interview and formally laid out the thoughts into paper so that we can define the strategic plan for it.

02 Define

After systematic documentation of requirements we started drilling various scenarios/workflows and defining the user experience around it. With CrowdSolveIt our main target was to reach the audience in a simplest possible manner and get most out of them. We cannot ignore that it has a complex mechanism and so important is to get it right. The team was in continuous discussion with the client defining each stage in an elaborative manner.

Paper Sketches
Lo-Fi Prototype

03 Design

The time has come to put the ideas on paper now. We created high fidelity interactive prototypes in axureRP preceding with paper prototypes and whiteboard designing. The designs were incrementally improvised based on the outcomes of different usability checks and regular discussion with clients. Once finalised team move forward to convert some masters into elegant visual designs.

Home Page Design
Project Discussion Page

04 Develop

  • Frontend Framework: Bootstrap 3.3.6 & AngularJS v1.5
  • Backend-Development: NodeJS v6.3.0
  • Database: MySQL v5.7.11

05 Deploy

CrowSolveIt has a web non-responsive user interface design supporting all screens above 1024x768 resolution. This is backed up on AWS servers with proper load balancing techniques to optimise the trafficking in extreme conditions.

06 Drive

The website was deployed with all the usability and quality checks. The designed was well defined and can be scaled to make it responsive on all form factors with half the efforts required. Additionally the website is powered by google analytics helping client to monitor all the data footprints.

StuderGroup

AN UX PROJECT BY SYNERZIP

Overview

StuderGroup, a private health care consulting group (acquired by Huron Consulting Group) has a number of web based applications which can be accessed through a single sign-in portal.

01 Discover

StuderGroup web portal is a collection of multiple independent applications that require single sign-in. These applications are typically accessed from a range of devices and browsers. Our primary goal was to design a simple and state of art user experience; supported by all legacy browsers (like IE 7) and devices (like 15' monitor and smartphones) to next generation handheld and fixed devices. After multiple customer interactions, careful study of resources and gap analysis, we came to the conclusion that this project will require us to define additional strategy to meet the desired goal.

02 Define

A complex enterprise application system needs to have a well defined primary, secondary and tertiary navigation. All patterns, navigation, transitions and icons have to be selected while keeping support for IE7 browser in mind. This does involve frequent interaction with customer, developers, UI development team and QAs to make sure everything is in perfect harmony.

Card Sorting Activity

03 Design

The design is user-centered, modern, clean, and full responsive in nature. FontAwesome 3.0 icons and neutral colors have been used while keeping accessibility in mind.

Desktop Design for StuderGroup Applications
Responsive Website

04 Develop

For IE7 Support, we could have used htc files and relied on javascript hacks, but we chose to define our own algorithm and box model that mimics twitter bootstrap grid layout for IE7 and 8. Yes, it took a considerable effort but performance on IE7 was dramatically improved by several seconds, which is an achievement in itself.

  • Frontend Framework: Bootstrap 3
  • Backend-Development: c# .NET

05 Deploy

The connection to StuderGroup uses a strong protocol version and cipher suite, hosted on secure cloud with full https connection and is supported by all browsers (including IE7). All applications are heavily tested and performance is constantly monitored.

06 Drive

We have used google analytics to keep a close watch on number of users, browser type, behavior and navigation pattern to learn, plan and further enhance applications in iterative manner. All applications are live and customer is happy. Yay !

Pune Plumbers

AN UX PROJECT BY SYNERZIP

Overview

Field service management (FSM) has significantly evolved in past few years. We at Synerzip, developed a field service management platform where an admin(s) can assign jobs and manage plumbers on field. Designing and building a complete system out of scratch was truly an overwhelming experience.

01 Discover

After drafting initial requirement specification document we started with discovery of common patterns and pain-points of existing FSM applications. We also interviewed multiple plumbers and encountered the fact that almost everyone is using smartphone and are pretty aware of apps like "Whatsapp, FaceBook and Hike".

02 Define

Our goal was clear and we were really confident about the planned approach, user persona and current market trends. The whole system has 3 major components : Admin Console, mobile app (ios and android) and smartwatch app.

Activity diagram of Admin Console.

After brainstorming, we started defining information architecture for all components independently, removed potential technology barrier after discussing with internal dev team and once everything was ready, we jumped on artboards to do what we all love. Designing.

03 Design

The admin console is fully responsive website where he can queue customer requests, prioritize them, assign them to plumbers suggested by system (based on their distance, work-load and ETAs). It based upon strict design principles and is amazingly easy to understand.

Activity diagram of Admin Console.
Activity diagram of Admin Console.

04 Develop

  • Frontend Framework: Bootstrap 3
  • Backend-Development: NodeJS
  • iOS and Android App: Swift 2.0 and native Android respectively

05 Deploy

Hosted on scalable AWS, facilitated with SMS gateways and push services. Validated and tested in live envrionment and conditions.

06 Drive

We have used google analytics and location services and usability of all apps have been verified multiple times.

Activity diagram of Admin Console.

This field service management solution is generic solution and can be scaled and tweaked according to any other business requirements like "food delivery service, taxi service etc."

Open Connect

AN UX PROJECT BY SYNERZIP

Overview

OpenConnect’s AutoiQ process automation solution is designed to automate routine tasks and workflows and improve first-pass efficiency AutoiQ is the latest generation of OpenConnect automation solutions that deliver value and benefits spanning multiple industries. For example: Healthcare insurance companies have significantly increased operational first-pass rate by automating the processing of a large number of additional claims beyond the capability of their primary claims adjudication application.

P&C insurance companies have reduced administrative costs, improved First Notice of Loss, reduced claims inventory, and improved quality and combined ratios.Customer service organizations have increased customer satisfaction and service by improving First Call Resolution (FCR),order entry, and provisioning processes.

01 Discover

While doing our primary research on RPA (Robotic Process Automation) and AutoiQ’s approach, we interviewed business analysts, system programmers and administrators to learn about their roles, responsibilities, level of comfort with existing systems. We also discovered major and minor pain-points, UX and usability issues for all available personas.

02 Define

AutoiQ is a rule based RPA system where UX is key differentiator. After definition of rules, process and schedules, robots carry out work on end machines and are constantly monitored via a centralized system that controls them. These robots are really fast and can replace manual, not-so-creative and time consuming jobs, thus saving millions of money every year. AutoiQ links with other homogenous and in-house developed products and has a powerful reporting engine.

Synerzip UX team has performed competitive analysis and thorough study of other RPA based systems to define a rock solid foundation; that is based upon core principles of RPA, adheres all standards and aims to deliver best user experience to its users.

03 Design

AutoiQ's new design is super simplified. Our job is to design something that helps users do their complicated work in smartest possible way and we always try and strive to do that.

AutoiQ is still in its primary phase of design and we will soon be pushing it to development team which take pride of pixel-perfection.

CMS

AN UX PROJECT BY SYNERZIP

Overview

Currently, many clinics in our country are still storing the patient’s record as physical documents which have their own time of expiry and prone to corrosion. Nurses needs to manually write down the patient’s information and index their medical records. These works are so troublesome and plaguing. Moreover, there is the concern of security even.

In the era of technology, where everything needs to be done efficiently and effectively the existences of Clinic Management System (CMS) become necessary. The use of CMS can enhance the services and also the workflow of all activity that happens in hospitals where it helps in reducing the workload of medical staff, the number of manpower needed and it also make hospitals management become more manageable, secure and easier to control.

The Clinic Management System is developed to digitize the daily operational activities in a clinic, before this is done manually. This system will involve all the clinic operation starting from patient registration until billing the patient. The important thing is it will become easier for the data record and retrieval. This system will be able to generate report regarding the clinic operation. The target user for this system is staff of the clinic, doctor and also the management. Above all the system developed for all major form factors viz web, mobile and tablet.

01 Discover

The first step was to discover the requirements of the product. So we did a detailed requirement analysis with the stakeholder while interviewing him for this project. We made some amazing discoveries on the basis of which we ran a small survey with different doctors in the city to get down the exact pain points. To give a differential stand to the project we did competitive analysis where we got to know the exact trends going in the market and what are the loopholes that can be addressed via this project.

02 Define

On the basis of our studies we drafted some documents which were discussed with the stakeholder and proposed an action plan for the project. Information Architecture and different Workflows were crafted with the team through various brainstorming sessions.

03 Design

After scratching their heads to crafting various page designs, the team started to get their hands dirty on realizing their thoughts through Photoshop. Before visual designs we assured that the thoughts are well aligned for the perfect solution to the user pain points, for which we did rapid prototyping in Axure and did Gorilla Testing with it. At every stage we keep in touch with the stakeholder and get his views on the designs.

High Fidelity Designs

04 Develop

  • Front-end Bootstrap 3.3.6 & AngularJS v1.4
  • Back-end NodeJS v5.10.1
  • Database MySQL v5.7
  • iOS & Android Mobile Native Apps

05 Deploy

Clinic Management System is developed for all the popular form factors to give a comprehensive support to the client. This system can be access via Web and all range of Mobiles and Tablets. In spite of going with hybrid, we have developed native application for a richer experience to the end user. All this is backed up on AWS servers with proper load balancing techniques to optimise the trafficking in extreme conditions.

06 Drive

The application is deployed with all the usability and quality checks. The designed was well engaged and providing a best user experience across all form factors.
